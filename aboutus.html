About Us

We are a manufacturer and exporter of Industrial Leather Gloves and Garments. We are situated in Kolkata, India, which is famous for its rich culture and heritage. Kolkata is also a manufacturing hub for leather and leather products.

We manufacture our leather under stringent quality control at the “Kolkata Leather Complex” in accordance with the latest “Reach Law” and produce gloves and garments in our factory with utmost care and sincerity, meeting EN 420 & 388 standards.

Our factory is equipped with clicking machines for accurate cutting of leather and modern sewing machines. After sewing, each glove is ironed to ensure desired shape and size and kept in a de-humidification chamber until it achieves the desired moisture level. Each product is packed inside air-conditioned rooms at controlled temperature to eliminate the risk of fungal growth during transit in the rainy season. Gloves are packed inside airtight poly packs and finally inside laminated cartons with the ability to withstand outside water.

We are a growing company exporting our products to Europe, North America, and South America, and we hope to expand our reputation further in the near future.
